About Me

The ocean and nature have always inspired me. I worked in marine science and botany for many years. I loved managing the Marine Science Department, but I found myself wanting to be more creative in my work. When I found UX/UI design, I knew it was a perfect fit, getting to know and understand people and create designs that meet their needs and goals sounded like the perfect career for me. Add in a little coding for the logical side of my brain, and it's even better. The University of Texas Trilogy Bootcamp course is fast paced and I've learned so much. I'm really enjoying the projects and can't wait to branch out further.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy swimming, scuba diving, and exploring the Big Island of Hawaii with my partner, Jeff, and my dogs, Quintana Roo and Wichita.
